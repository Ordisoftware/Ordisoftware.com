Software programming

    

Overview

Software solutions

Through targeted automation and adapted consideration of the interaction of flows and processings within your activity, a simple, effective and efficient software solution can be implemented in autonomous, client-serveur, Intranet and Internet architectures:

  • A utility to manage your passions, your leisures or your association.
  • A process to manage your customers, your services and your stocks.
  • A tool to manage your stores, your products, your sales and your invoicings.
  • A software to organize the activity of your company and your collaborators.
  • A system to control the interaction between your machines and their users.
  • A supervisor for your manufacturing methods and your procedures.
  • A system or a database component.

Domains of intervention

Ordisoftware™ act mainly on the technical level within all spheres of civil activity, in particular these following business, except the army and the police:

  • Agriculture, Agroalimentary, Biotechnology
  • Art, Culture, Media, Audiovisual
  • Bank, Insurance, Finance
  • Commerce, Crafts, Sale, Distribution, Logistics
  • Teaching, Education, Social, Justice
  • Estate, Hotel, Restoration
  • Industry, Chemistry, Data processing, Telecommunication
  • Information, Documentation, Edition, Press
  • Health, Medical, Paramedical, Cosmetic
  • Sport, Leisure, Tourism, Travel

Whether you are a private individual, an artist, an association, a merchant, an artisan, a company, an administration, a school, a solo professional or a group.
